The Legacy from then to the future

Founded in 1976, CGI is a global IT and business process services provider delivering high-quality business consulting, systems integration and outsourcing services. With 70,000 professionals serving clients from hundreds of locations worldwide, CGI has an industry-leading track record of on-time, on-budget projects, aligning our teams with clients’ business strategies to achieve top-to-bottom line results.

Leaders

People who have been carrying out the legacy since its inception

Founder of CGI

Serge Godin

Serge Godin comes from a family of 9 children. It is his father, whom he describes as "extraordinary entrepreneur", who has given him the taste to go into business. 1 Serge Godin was educated at the Collège de Jonquière where he obtained one of the first college diplomas in computer science in Quebec. He also holds a training in administration, acquired at Université Laval.

Co-Founder of CGI

André Imbeau

Mr. André Imbeau serves as the President of Groupe Gestion G5 Inc. Mr. Imbeau co-founded CGI Group Inc. in 1976 and served as its Advisor to the Founder & Executive Chairman of the Board since July 2006 until October 1, 2016. Mr. Imbeau served as Treasurer and of CGI Group Inc. since September 2006. He served as Corporate Secretary of CGI Group Inc. since July 2006 until August 1, 2015

Partners

CGI is a Globally Managed Partner for Microsoft, as well as a Microsoft Gold or Silver partner in over 20 different competencies. We also have one of the largest suite of Microsoft offerings in the market, as well as 8,000+ dedicated Microsoft professionals in 25+ countries. Learn more about our partnership with Microsoft.

CGI is an Oracle Global Platinum Partner, specializing in a wide range of areas, including applications and systems, partner governance, joint global initiatives, business integration platform services, and Oracle-based solutions (30+) within the public sector, retail, telecom and health sectors. CGI is also an Oracle client, running Hyperion, Sales Cloud, and PeopleSoft within our own company.

As a SAP partner, CGI specializes in more than 25 SAP areas—from application management, to SuccessFactor talent management software, to HANA and mobility, to industry solutions in financial services, manufacturing, health and utilities.
